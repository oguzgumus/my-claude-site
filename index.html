About

Hello, I'm Ozzy

My name is Oğuzhan Gümüş — most people just call me Ozzy. I'm a photographer based in Barcelona, working in portraits, travel and editorial photography for private clients and brands alike.

I grew up in Türkiye and still shoot there often, so I move easily between Barcelona, the rest of Europe and back home. My approach is warm and unhurried: I want the people and places I photograph to look like themselves on their best day.

Available for commissions and collaborations. I work in English, Spanish and Turkish — reach out in whichever feels natural.
